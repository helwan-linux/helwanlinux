Helwan Command Runner Guide

A simple arcade-style game designed for the Helwan Linux environment.

How to Play

  • Objective: Survive for as long as possible by avoiding and destroying obstacles.
  • Grey Blocks: These are solid obstacles. If you touch them, the game ends immediately.
  • Red Blocks: These are targets that can be destroyed to clear your path.

Controls

  • Left/Right Arrows: Move your runner between lanes to dodge blocks.
  • Spacebar: Shoot to destroy Red Blocks in front of you.
  • R Key: Restart the game after a Game Over.
  • Q Key: Quit the game.

Quick Tips

Focus on the Grey Blocks first; they are your main threat. The game speed increases over time, so keep your eyes on the track!
