Helwan Solitaire: Open Source Edition

A tribute to the pioneers who built the digital world.

What is Helwan Solitaire?

Helwan Solitaire is a customized version of the classic card game, developed specifically for Helwan Linux. While it retains the familiar Klondike rules, it serves a higher purpose: educating users about the history of Free and Open Source Software (FOSS).

Why Play This Edition?

  • The Geniuses Deck: Instead of standard royalty, the cards feature legendary figures like Linus Torvalds and Richard Stallman.
  • Interactive Learning: Hovering over cards reveals brief biographies, turning a simple game into a history lesson.
  • Native & Lightweight: Built with Python and Pygame for optimal performance on global distributions.
  • Privacy First: Fully offline, with no tracking or data collection, following the Helwan security standards.

Credits and Ethics

We believe in professional collaboration. This project is a derivative work of Solitaire-Pygame by Kallekro. Full permission was requested and documented to ensure the highest ethical standards in our global distribution.

How to Install on Helwan Linux

Open your terminal and use the hpm package manager:

hpm install hel-solitaire
