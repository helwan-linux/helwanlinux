What is Momo?

Think of **Momo** as a digital doctor for your computer. Instead of typing complex commands to find out why your PC is slow or if your hardware is healthy, Momo gives you a simple menu to check everything in one place.

How to start?

You don't need to look for files or folders. Just open your Terminal and type one simple word:

momo

That's it! The "Doctor" is now ready to assist you .

What can Momo do for you?

  • Check Health: See if your hard drive or SSD is healthy or needs replacement.
  • Test Speed: Measure how fast your internet and memory (RAM) really are .
  • Stress Test: Make sure your computer won't crash when you do heavy work or gaming.
  • Temperature Monitor: Check if your device is getting too hot.

Peace of Mind

Everything Momo finds is saved in a report file in your home folder (~/.momo/logs). If you ever need help from the Helwan community, you can just send them these logs.
